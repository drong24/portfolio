About

Hello! My name is Daisy and I am a senior at Auburn University studying Computer Science. One of the biggest draws I find in programming is being able to build whatever you want from just an idea. I also love the process of breaking down a problem and figuring out how it all works. I mainly choose to code in Java and Javascript (the two languages I'm most comfortable with), as well as C# and Python.
